== Description ==

### WordPress MUSIC PLAYER Plugin

Easily embed your favourite music player on your site by using **"Music Player" block**. No shortcodes, no manual work. The controls can be found within the block. By default, the music player automatically plays all the media files found on your media library.

Comes with 10 different music player skin/themes you can choose from.
